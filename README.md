# Fashion Store Sales Analysis Using SQL

## Overview

This project analyzes sales data from the online fashion store "LakuBanyak" using SQL. The analysis focuses on product performance, sales trends, and business insights to support data-driven decision-making.

## Case Study

LakuBanyak is an online fashion store that sells clothing, shoes, bags, and accessories. This project aims to analyze sales data by:

1. Exploring product data.
2. Filtering products by category.
3. Calculating the total number of products.
4. Calculating total sales revenue.
5. Analyzing sales performance by product category.
6. Identifying the top-selling products.

## Key Findings

- The store offers 15 different products across multiple categories.
- Total sales revenue reached Rp 6,866,000.
- The Shoes category generated the highest sales revenue (Rp 2,275,000).
- Kaos Polos Hitam was the best-selling product, followed by Topi Baseball and Kemeja Flanel.

## Recommendations

- Increase marketing efforts and product variety for the Shoes category due to its strong revenue performance.
- Maintain sufficient stock of Kaos Polos Hitam and highlight it in promotional campaigns to attract more customers.

## Tools Used

- SQL (MySQL)
